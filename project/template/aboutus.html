About Us

At Cycle Share, we strive to provide an effortless, eco-friendly solution for campus mobility. Our platform enables students to rent and share bicycles with peers, fostering a culture of sustainability, affordability, and convenience. We are dedicated to promoting healthier lifestyles and reducing our carbon footprint one ride at a time. Join us in this journey and make your daily commutes more enjoyable and impactful.
